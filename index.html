Why use Bootstrap?

Since the introduction of Bootstrap earlier in the semester, I've been eager to experiment with it on one of my web projects. Initially, I hesitated because our class projects were mostly group-oriented, and I didn't want to stray from the familiar tools our team was using. However, as the semester draws to a close, I've chosen to embrace Bootstrap for our final project. Its user-friendly nature caught my attention from the start, and it seems ideal for adding the necessary responsiveness to my portfolio site. With Bootstrapâ€™s built-in features, I can focus less on technical challenges and more on creating a distinct and inspired design.
